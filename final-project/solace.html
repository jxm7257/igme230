Solace

This semester, I spent 12 weeks researching how to make the medical experience more informative and personalized to it's patients, and their families. My solution is an application called Solace that is meant to help the user educate themselves and manage their own treatment.

My Approach

I centered my design around a user that had just been diagnosed and was using this applicaiton to help feel more prepared. It would sync with their account all patients are given when they are in a hospital, so new perscriptions and suggestions appointments would auto-populate. That way, the user can just focus on getting better.
