DMV Form

Traditional DMV Forms are tedious and inconvenient. In an effort to make a dull experience a little more enjoyable, I explored what this form would look like in an interactive, online space.

My Approach

My goal was to make this a personalized and enjoyable experience. Information and different sections would be generated based on the user's responses. That way, if there is content that doesn't pertain to them, it's not visible unless it's necessary. This was meant to elimate clutter and keep the user focused on their one goal at hand.
