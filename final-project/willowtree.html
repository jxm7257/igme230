Willowtree Animation

Over the summer, I worked to create a branding animation for Willowtree, Inc. The result was a thirty second animation illustrating their mission statement "The freedom to create products people love."

My Approach

After lots of sketching and inspiration searching, I realized I wanted to do something that captured the fun-loving nature of the company while highlighting the complexity of the creative design process. Because this is a mobile developement agency, I also wanted to incorporate the technical side of things, and highlight the broad mobile network that this technology allows us to reach.
