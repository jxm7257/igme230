DMV Form

Nobody enjoys going to the DMV. It's unfriendly, stuck in the past, and overall an experience that everyone dreads. This vehicle registration form redesign was meant to take an out-of-date form and bring it into the modern tech world. Focusing on making the entire experience more enjoyable and humanistic, I had to rethink how the form would be responsive to user-input. Thus, simplifying the experience as a whole.

Summary
